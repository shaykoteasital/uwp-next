A Clear Vision for Saint Lucia

The United Workers Party is committed to building a Saint Lucia where every citizen can thrive. Our vision is rooted in trust, transparency, and putting people first—creating opportunities for growth, security, and prosperity for all.

The Current Reality

While others offered slogans and style, the UWP delivered substance and results. Under previous leadership, Saint Lucia saw record investments, infrastructure development, and economic growth.

  • Broken promises and failed policies
  • Rising costs with no relief in sight
  • Deteriorating public services and infrastructure

The UWP Promise

We're committed to reclaiming that legacy—restoring trust, delivering relief, and building a modern economy that works for everyone, especially our youth and most vulnerable.

  • Trust and integrity in every action
  • Real relief through the SOS Plan
  • Modern infrastructure and sustainable growth

3 Strategic Objectives

Our approach is clear, focused, and actionable. These three strategic pillars guide every policy, every decision, and every action we take.

01

Rebuild Trust & Restore Confidence

Establish transparent governance, enforce accountability, and rebuild public trust through integrity and ethical leadership.

  • Strengthen anti-corruption measures
  • Implement transparent procurement systems
  • Enhance oversight and accountability
  • Restore public confidence in institutions
02

Deliver Immediate Relief Through SOS Plan

Provide direct, measurable relief to families through our comprehensive Salvation, Opportunity & Security initiative.

  • Reduce cost of living with targeted subsidies
  • Create jobs and economic opportunities
  • Enhance public safety and security
  • Support vulnerable groups and families
03

Build a Modern, Diversified Economy

Invest in infrastructure, technology, and innovation to create sustainable growth across all sectors.

  • Modernize agriculture and agro-processing
  • Expand digital and creative industries
  • Develop sustainable tourism
  • Invest in education, healthcare, and infrastructure

Priority Policy Areas

Our manifesto addresses the issues that matter most to Saint Lucians—from security and healthcare to economic growth and environmental sustainability.

Security & Justice

Strengthen law enforcement, enhance community safety, and reform the justice system.

  • Increase police presence and resources
  • Community policing initiatives
  • Justice system modernization

Economic Growth

Build a modern, diversified economy across agriculture, tourism, digital, and creative sectors.

  • Agriculture modernization
  • Digital economy expansion
  • Tourism diversification

Healthcare

Improve healthcare access, quality, and infrastructure for all citizens.

  • Complete St. Jude Hospital
  • Expand primary care facilities
  • Mental health services

Education

Invest in quality education, skills training, and lifelong learning opportunities.

  • Upgrade school infrastructure
  • Teacher training and support
  • Vocational and technical education

Infrastructure

Develop modern infrastructure including roads, ports, water, and digital connectivity.

  • Road network improvement
  • Water infrastructure upgrades
  • Broadband expansion

Environment & Energy

Transition to renewable energy and protect our natural environment for future generations.

  • Renewable energy projects
  • Climate adaptation measures
  • Environmental protection

Each priority area is backed by detailed policies and concrete action plans in our full manifesto.
